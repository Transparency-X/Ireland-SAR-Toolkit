# Legal Basis for SARs in Ireland

## GDPR Article 15
- **Right of Access**: Data subjects have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether or not personal data concerning them are being processed, and, where that is the case, access to the personal data.
- **Scope**: Applies to all controllers and processors of personal data, except where exemptions apply (e.g., national security).
- **Response Time**: 1 calendar month (extendable by 2 further months for complex/large requests).
- **Cost**: Free of charge.

## Data Protection Act 2018, Part 3 (Law Enforcement Directive)
- **Section 89**: Right of access to personal data held by An Garda Síochána.
- **Section 91**: Right of access to personal data held by public bodies.
- **Exemptions**: Broader than standard GDPR. Data may be withheld if disclosure would prejudice an ongoing criminal investigation, endanger life, or interfere with operations.

## Key Differences
| Aspect | GDPR Art. 15 | DPA 2018 Part 3 |
|---|---|---|
| Applies to | All controllers | Gardaí, designated public bodies |
| Response Time | 1 month | 1 month |
| Fees | Free (unless manifestly unfounded/excessive) | Free |
| Enforcement | Data Protection Commission (DPC) | DPC |
| Exemptions | Limited | Broader for law enforcement data |
